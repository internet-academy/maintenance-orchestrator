## 🎯 OBJECTIVE

## 🛠️ TECHNICAL CRITERIA
- **Technical Rationale**: Explain *why* each method works (e.g., API response timing, social media account linking).
- **Privacy & Legal Context**: Mention the ethical and legal boundaries (GDPR, Terms of Service) for each method.

## 🔍 QUALITY AUDIT (SENTRY)

- **Safety**: Ensure no instructions are provided that encourage illegal hacking or harassment.
